Before real estate stole my heart, I lived a few different lives.

From Politics to PR

I graduated in 2010 with a degree in Political Science, then followed it up with a postgrad diploma in Public Relations and Management from McGill.

A Career in Marketing

From there, I dove headfirst into the world of marketing, 15 years of it! I spent 7 of those years leading incredible teams as Head of Marketing, including one of my proudest roles: launching and leading the marketing for Qatar Airways in Montreal for six amazing years. I’ve also had the joy of working in public service with the City of Montreal and spent a few fast-paced years in SaaS. I'm now looking forward to apply my mark-tech experience to the world of real estate.
